================================================================================
KEY INNOVATIONS
================================================================================

1. PCAT (Physics-Constrained Attention)
   - First implementation of thermodynamic constraints in attention softmax
   - Each of 8 heads respects one physics domain
   - Physics penalty network predicts constraints on-the-fly

2. HB-SSM (Hierarchical Bayesian State-Space)
   - 4-level hierarchy capturing degradation at multiple timescales
   - Coupled Kalman filters across levels
   - Rauch smoother for backward refinement

3. CDGM (Causal Degradation Graphical Model)
   - PC algorithm for causal structure learning
   - Do-calculus for counterfactual reasoning
   - Physics-based edge orientation

4. MALADAPT (Meta-Learning Adaptation)
   - MAML-style approach for 1-2 step airline customization
   - Enables handling of 5 distinct Indian airline profiles
   - Model-agnostic meta-learning framework

5. HP-NODE (Hybrid Physics-Data ODE)
   - Splits dynamics: known physics + learned residuals
   - Combines interpretability with flexibility
   - Captures rare exceptions missed by pure physics models

6. MMUF (Multi-Modal Uncertainty Fusion)
   - Fuses 4 uncertainty sources
   - Aleatoric + Epistemic + Drift + Physics
   - Structured kernel-based combination

7. CPAL (Contrastive Physics-Aware Learning)
   - Triplet loss with physics consistency penalties
   - Ensures ensemble coordination
   - Physics-aware embedding space

8. SuperEnsemble
   - Unified prediction combining all 7 methods
   - Learned fusion weights for optimal accuracy stacking
   - Proper uncertainty propagation through ensemble
